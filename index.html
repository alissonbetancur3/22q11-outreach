What Is 22q11.2 Deletion Syndrome?

22q11.2 Deletion Syndrome is a genetic condition caused by a small missing piece of chromosome 22. It affects each person differently and can impact multiple systems in the body.

Symptoms

  • Heart defects
  • Speech delays
  • Learning challenges
  • Immune system differences
  • Feeding or growth issues

Diagnosis

This condition is diagnosed through genetic testing, usually with a blood test. It may be identified before birth or during childhood.

Treatment & Support

While there is no cure, treatments such as medical care, therapy, and educational support can help individuals manage symptoms and live healthy lives.
